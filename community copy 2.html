MLCCC Board of Directors

In November 2008, the MLCCC received a grant from the Shirley and Morrison C. Boyd Fund of The Philadelphia Foundation. Part of the funding has been used to build a formal Board of Directors. The volunteer group represents a number of Greater Philadelphia community members who come from diverse backgrounds but share common excellence in terms of business and academic profiles.

Sue Cheng

  • Founder, ABD International, LLC, an international business development company
  • Owner and President, Easternwise Medical Inc., a global company dedicated to providing World-Class service to hospitals, diagnostic imaging centers and private clinics in the United States and around the world
  • 10+ years of experience in IT project management, Software Engineering, Mechanical Engineering at Siemens, Baja Systems and Huazhong Optoelectronic Technology Institute
  • MS in Computer Science, West Chester University of Pennsylvania
  • MS in Mechanical Engineering, University of Massachusetts at Amherst
  • B.S in Mechanical Engineering, Sichuan University

Terry Cooke

  • Current 2010 Public Policy Scholar with the Woodrow Wilson International Center in Washington DC, researching policy implications of US-China clean energy cooperation/competition
  • Founder of GC3 Strategy, which works with US companies seeking to partner in Greater China
  • Director of the World Economic Forum (2006-2008), responsible for Asian Corporate Partnerships
  • Career member of the US Senior Foreign Commercial Service (1988-2002)
  • UC Berkeley, PhD and MA; Princeton University, BA
  • Diplomatic fluency in Mandarin Chinese, Japanese, Hindi, German and French
