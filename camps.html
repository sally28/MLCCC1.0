MLCCC Summer Camp Programs

MLCCC is offering the following summer camps for 2020. Registration has begun for all of them.

  1. MLCCC Chinese Summer Camp in Wayne, PA
  2. MLCCC English Writing Summer Camp in Wayne,PA
  3. MLCCC Math Summer Camp in Wayne, PA
  4. MLCCC Fencing Summer Camp in Wayne, PA

Our Chinese Language and Culture Summer Camp is open to children from 3 to 13 years of age. Teachers are native Mandarin speakers and the curriculum is designed to broaden the children's understanding and appreciation of Chinese language, culture, history, arts and customs in an engaging and effective way. This is a great opportunity for your child to explore other areas of interest beyond his or her normal schooling with a global perspective.

We provide many fun activities including culture lessons, songs, dance, drawing, crafts, games, karate, and outdoors fun, all set within a Mandarin language learning environment. The camps offer the flexibility of registering for multi-week sessions or individual weeks, as well as full days or partial days. At the end of each multi-week session, campers will have an opportunity to present what they have learned to their parents by being in a camp-wide show.

Our English Writing Summer Camp is open to all students entering 3rd, 4th, 5th, or 6th grade in September. It is taught by a Pennsylvania certified K-12 Language Arts teacher. The purpose is to improve writing skills, hone thinking abilities, and foster a confidence and fondness for words and language. Topics will include:

  • various forms of writing such as narrative, persuasive, poetic, etc.
  • literary language such as similes, metaphors, etc.
  • writing techniques and organization.

Our Math Summer Camp is open to all students entering 3rd, 4th, 5th, or 6th grade in September, and run in a similar manner as the Writing Camp.

Our Fencing Camp is open to children ages 7-14 and taught by a certified fencing coach.

Our English Writing Camp, Math Camp, and Fencing Camp are within the same building as our Chinese Language and Culture Camp at Wayne, and are 3 hours a day for 2 weeks. Hence students at our Wayne location have the option to register for:

  • just our Chinese Camp, or
  • just our English Writing Camp, or
  • just our Math Enrichment Camp, or
  • just our Fencing Camp, or
  • a combo of two of the above (example: English Writing in the morning and Math in the afternoon, or Chinese in the morning and Fencing in the afternoon, all with the option of after care
MLCCC Chinese Summer Camp in Wayne, PA

Located at the United Church Of Christ at Valley Forge (map), at 45 Walker Road, Wayne, PA 19087, near Valley Forge Elementary School, our MLCCC summer camp is open to children ages 3-13 and provides a variety of language and cultural activities that enrich and entertain our campers' summer days.

Our Chinese Camp includes: Chinese Language and Cultural Lessons and Projects, Drawing, Karate, Chinese Folk Dance, Math, Games, and Physical Education. Please see a sample of our weekly schedule to get an idea of what our camp is like. We also provide "Special Classes" such as Tennis and Martial Arts, which are taught by expert teachers that we invite to our school. Children who are not enrolled in our camp are also welcome to join these classes for an additional fee.

We offer the flexibility of registering for two-week sessions at a discount or for individual weeks, as well as the flexibility of registering for full days (8am - 6pm), ¾ days (8am - 3:30pm), or half days (8am - 1pm). We also have sibling and return camper discounts, in addition to early registration and referral bonus.

Since our English Writing Camp, Math Enrichment Camp and Fencing Camp are also at the same location and each runs for 3 hours each day in a 2-week session, our Wayne campers will have the option to mix and match the four choices: Chinese, English Writing, Math or Fencing. For example, they can be in Writing Camp for half the day and Math Camp for half the day, or Fencing Camp for half the day and Chinese Camp for the rest of the day.
Please see our Chinese, English Writing,Math Enrichment and Fencing Camps Combo Registration Form.

MLCCC Enrichment Summer Camps in Wayne, PA

Located at the United Church Of Christ at Valley Forge (map), at 45 Walker Road, Wayne, PA 19087, near Valley Forge Elementary School.

English Writing Summer Camp

Our English Writing camp is for all students in 3rd, 4th, 5th, or 6th grades in September. It is taught by a Pennsylvania certified K-12 Language Arts teacher and runs for 3 hours a day for 10 days (2 weeks).

The purpose of the camp is to improve writing skills, thinking abilities, and inspire confidence and love for the written word. A sample of topics taught include:

  • essay forms (such as narrative, persuasive, descriptive) and creative writing.
  • writing analysis, reading analysis.
  • vocabulary building, grammar, and writing mechanics.
Math Enrichment Camp

Our Math camp is for all students in 3rd, 4th, 5th, or 6th grades in September. It is taught by a college professor and runs for 3 hours a day for 10 days (2 weeks).

The purpose of the camp is to reinforce the students' understanding of math concepts, and improve critical thinking and problem solving skills. Examples of possible topics are:

  • arithmetic, graphs, fractions, geometry for younger class;
  • negative numbers, decimals, ratios, percents, basic algebra for the older class.
Fencing Camp

Our Fencing camp is for students aged 7-14.It runs for 3 hours a day for 2 weeks. Equipment is provided by camp. It is taught by the head fencing coach of a YMCA with 20+ years of experience. Several of his students made Japan’s Fencing Team for the 2020 Olympics.

This program uses a variety of games that promote strength, coordination, and tactical understanding. Students are grouped by level. Beginner group covers foot technique, proper use/handling of the foil sword, and includes mock bouts. Intermediate group hones the techniques and skills further, into competitive style fencing and allows the fencer to test his/her limits. The class emphasizes having fun while practicing discipline, acquiring strength and improving gross motor skills.

We have sibling and return camper discounts, in addition to early registration and referral bonus.

Since our English Writing Camp, Math Enrichment Camp and Fencing Camp are also at the same location and each runs for 3 hours each day in a 2-week session, our Wayne campers will have the option to mix and match the four choices: Chinese, English Writing, Math or Fencing. For example, they can be in Writing Camp for half the day and Math Camp for half the day, or Fencing Camp for half the day and Chinese Camp for the rest of the day.
Please see our Chinese, English Writing,Math Enrichment and Fencing Camps Combo Registration Form.

MLCCC Summer Camo Policies

Registration Fee:
$50 per family, waived if deposit is submitted or postmarked by 4/1/2020.

Referral Bouns Policy:
Referral Bonus: $25 bonus will be given for each new camper referred (from different family) who registers for at least 2 weeks (new = have not attended any of our camps before). There is no limit on the number of referrals.

Late Pickup Policy:
$1 per minute late fee.

Deposit Policy:
Chinese Camp: $60 per week, $120 per session, $540 for the whole summer.
Enrichment/Combo Camp: $150 per session, $600 for the whole summer.
Deposit is required for each child at registration time.
Deposit is fully refundable if withdraw by 4/1/2020.After 4/1/2020, deposit is non-refundable.

Policy on Switching Weeks, Changing Pick Up Times, or Adding Weeks (If Space is Available)
Before or on 6/1/2020, no cost to switching weeks, changing pick up times, or adding weeks.
After 6/1/2020, a $5 fee per child per transaction, for each switch-of-weeks / change-of-pick-up-times (even if for a day) / addition-of-weeks.
Note that policy on reducing number of weeks or withdrawing completely is specified below.
