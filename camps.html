MLCCC offers a Chinese Language and Culture Camp, an English Writing Camp, a Math Camp, and more. Registration for the 2020 Chinese Language and Culture Camp has begun. Registration for English Writing and Math Camps will begin in February. Other special camps such as International Chess are being considered for the 2020 season.

  1. MLCCC Chinese Summer Camp in Wayne, PA
  2. MLCCC English Writing Summer Camp in Wayne,PA
  3. MLCCC Math Enrichment,MATHCOUNTS Summer Camp in Wayne, PA
  4. MLCCC Martial Arts / Self Defense and Fencing Summer Camp in Wayne, PA

Our Chinese Language and Culture Summer Camp is open to children from 3 to 13 years of age. Teachers are native Mandarin speakers and the curriculum is designed to broaden the children's understanding and appreciation of Chinese language, culture, history, arts and customs in an engaging and effective way. This is a great opportunity for your child to explore other areas of interest beyond his or her normal schooling with a global perspective.

We provide many fun activities including culture lessons, songs, dance, drawing, crafts, games, karate, and outdoors fun, all set within a Mandarin language learning environment. The camps offer the flexibility of registering for multi-week sessions or individual weeks, as well as full days or partial days. At the end of each multi-week session, campers will have an opportunity to present what they have learned to their parents by being in a camp-wide show.

Our English Writing Summer Camp is open to all students entering 3rd, 4th, 5th, or 6th grade in September. It is taught by a Pennsylvania certified K-12 Language Arts teacher. The purpose is to improve writing skills, hone thinking abilities, and foster a confidence and fondness for words and language. Topics will include:

  • various forms of writing such as narrative, persuasive, poetic, etc.
  • literary language such as similes, metaphors, etc.
  • writing techniques and organization.

Our Math Enrichment Summer Camp is open to all students entering 3rd, 4th, 5th, or 6th grade in September, and run in a similar manner as the Writing Camp. Our English Writing Camp, Math Enrichment Camp, and Fencing Camp are within the same building as our Chinese Language and Culture Camp at Wayne, and are 3 hours a day for 2 weeks. Hence students at our Wayne location have the option to register for:

  • just our Chinese Camp, or
  • just our English Writing Camp, or
  • just our Math Enrichment Camp, or
  • just our Fencing Camp, or
  • a combo of two of the above (example: English Writing in the morning and Math in the afternoon, or Chinese in the morning and Fencing in the afternoon, all with the option of after care

MLCCC Chinese Summer Camp in Wayne, PA

Located at the United Church Of Christ at Valley Forge (map), at 45 Walker Road, Wayne, PA 19087, near Valley Forge Elementary School, our MLCCC summer camp is open to children ages 3-13 and provides a variety of language and cultural activities that enrich and entertain our campers' summer days.

Our Chinese Camp includes: Chinese Language and Cultural Lessons and Projects, Drawing, Karate, Chinese Folk Dance, Math, Games, and Physical Education. Children who are not enrolled in our camp are also welcome to join these classes for an additional fee.

MLCCC English Writing Summer Camp in Wayne, PA

Located at the United Church Of Christ at Valley Forge (map), at 45 Walker Road, Wayne, PA 19087, near Valley Forge Elementary School, our MLCCC English Writing summer camp is open to all students who will be 3rd, 4th, 5th, or 6th graders in September. It is taught by a Pennsylvania certified K-12 Language Arts teacher and runs for 3 hours a day for 10 days (2 weeks).

The purpose of the camp is not only to improve writing skills, but also to enhance thinking abilities, and inspire confidence and love for the power of the written word and language. Topics taught include:

  • various forms of writing such as narrative, persuasive, poetic, etc.
  • literary language such as similes, metaphors, etc.
  • writing mechanics, techniques and organization.

We have sibling and return camper discounts, in addition to early registration and referral bonus.

Since our English Writing Camp, Math Enrichment Camp and Fencing Camp are also at the same location and each runs for 3 hours each day in a 2-week session, our Wayne campers will have the option to mix and match the four choices: Chinese, English Writing, Math or Fencing. For example, they can be in Writing Camp for half the day and Math Camp for half the day, or Fencing Camp for half the day and Chinese Camp for the rest of the day. Please see our Chinese, English Writing, Math Enrichment and Fencing Camps Combo Registration Form.
