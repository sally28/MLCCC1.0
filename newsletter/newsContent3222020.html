C. Penn Medicine Healthcare Career Exploration for High School Students

Do you have a child in high school that is interested in the healthcare field? Penn Medicine has partnered with the Cradle of Liberty Council of Scouts to launch an Explorers Post. Meetings are for local high school students to learn from Penn Medicine employees about different ways they can pursue a career in healthcare beyond the notion that a student can only be a doctor or nurse. Through this partnership, the Penn Medicine Explorers Post hosts career exploration meetings once a month, highlighting different career opportunities in healthcare.

Meeting Schedule:

Our monthly meetings occur the last Tuesday of each month from 5:00pm-6:30pm on the Penn Presbyterian Medical Center (PPMC) Campus in the Philadelphia Heart Institute (PHI) Auditorium. Below is our schedule for the rest of the school year.

February – Information Services/Information Technology

March – Nursing

April – Medical Simulation Education (tentative)

May – Paramedic/EMT/PennSTAR (tentative)

C. Call for Volunteer English Tutors for Children of Medical Staff in Wuhan

Let Love Guide Hope, Provide Free Online English Lessons for the Children Of Medical Staff In China. 

Three MLCCC TAs, Elizabeth Doyle, Cindy Lu, and Nikki Cao have completed the online training and certified to begin tutor online, congratulations!

中国疫区医护子女在线英语志愿教师招募书 - 让爱守护希望，为中国疫区医护子女提供免费英语在线辅导.

Elizabeth Doyle， Cindy Lu and Nikki Cao. Congratulations!

 

If you are 13 years or older and are looking for a great way to show that you truly care about people of Wuhan plagued by the new Coronavirus, in particular, the children of the doctors and nurses who are risking their lives saving patients’ lives, here is your opportunity. 

 

The Chinese School Association in the US (CSAUS) is looking for a few dedicated young and talented English tutors to teach those children English online. Once you sign up, you will receive instruction to get trained, and certified before your first lesson. 

Join this great cause, make a difference in those children’s lives while expanding your own horizon.

Sign up online here, and then follow the instructions here to prepare yourself for the first online tutoring lesson.
