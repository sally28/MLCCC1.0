Please note the following grading rules for Round 2.  Try not to lose points because speech is longer than 3:00 minutes.  

1. Pronunciation/发音 (20分): pronunciation of the spoken words.

2. Content/演讲内容 (20分): if the theme is appropriate for the audience, appropriate use of words.

3. Fluency/流畅 (20分): fluidity of the speech (beginning, transition, ending).

4. Tone/声调 (20分): the tone of the spoken words and sentences.

5. Presentation/演讲仪表 (20分): facial expressions, eye contact, body language, attire, and how presenter starts and ends the speech; whether a speaker has your attention during the speech.

6. Scoring rules on length of speech, and possible deductions:

- Each speech should be less than 3 minutes.  

- Length of speech measures from when contestant starts telling the story.

- If the speech is longer than 3 minutes but shorter than 3 minutes 30 seconds, a 3-point deduction applies.

- If the speech is longer than 3 minutes 30 seconds, a 5-point deduction applies.

 

To maintain consistency and fairness, similar to Round 1, a judge will not grade his/her own child; and a group of contestants will be graded only by their group of judges.
