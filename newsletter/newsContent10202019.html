3. Halloween Parade on 10/27, 2:45PM-3:05PM

Per PTO’s creativity, this year for our Halloween celebration, instead of crowding in the Admin Hallway, we will organize a parade by our children around the school hallways (probably from Admin Hallway to Main Entrance Hallway and then back) so that we can all enjoy the costumes better and take more photos!  The event will still take place during extra-long recess 2:45PM-3:05PM, and will end with trick-or-treating.       

 

4. “Best Costume” Awards

We will be having a “Best Costume” competition again this year.  Last year’s adult costumes, such as the blind fortune teller, red fox, and emperor, were fantastic!  We encourage everyone to be creative in their costumes. It can be typical Halloween, Chinese heritage, or MLCCC spirit! To Page Top

 

5. NEW Activity: Birthday Recognition

In order to make our school community more fun for our children, the ladies of our School Store came up with the idea of PTO and Admin teaming up to recognize the birthdays of our students. When the month of a child’s birthday comes around, the child can pick up a gift/prize at the School Store. Parents can also write or record a greeting that will be announced or played on 明朗广播电台 MLCCC Radio (link is also at the bottom of this newsletter). Summer birthdays will be celebrated in May. For privacy concerns, we only need the birth month, not year nor day. If you would like to try this out, please complete the simple sign up here.
