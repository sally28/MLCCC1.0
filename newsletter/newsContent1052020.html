1. Classes Resume This Sunday, 1/5/2020, Welcome Back

We hope everyone had a rested winter break and are ready to resume the fun of learning. Please check our school calendar for the upcoming major school-wide events, in particular, our 2020 Chinese New Year Gala and Culture Fair.

 

2. Midterm Exam This Sunday for MZHY Classes

Most MZHY classes are having their midterm exam this Sunday.  Best of luck to our students!

4. Chinese New Year Gala on 1/26/2020 - Sponsors Sought

Our Gala and Culture Fair has been a main cultural event on the western Philadelphia Suburbs since 2003 and has attracted hundreds of families of all walks of life, it is a great event for the community to come together and enjoy a day of rich cultural experience. It is also a great platform for local businesses to reach out to our community and beyond. See a wrapup of last year’s Gala event. Interested sponsors please contact us or sign up online here by January 10. Thank you!

 

You can also promote our event by sharing the 2020 Gala Flyer and our invitation letter with friends, coworkers, and neighbors. Thank you!
