Where to Sit in the Auditorium
The children (and their parents) of the same act should sit together, to make gathering students to go onto stage faster.  We will mark the seats. Children are their parents’ responsibility. Each act will line up to go onstage 3 acts before its own performance.       

Slide Shows and Videos

Through the hard work and creativity of our Video Club, presentation of this year’s Show is more hi-tech and more professional than all prior Galas!  Thank you to our renown MLCCC Club166!

 

Front of Stage No Standing - Safety!

There will be many wires, plugs, and equipment in front of the center of the stage.  Therefore for our children’s safety, the area in front of the center stage will be off-limits.  Children can no longer stand with their bodies touching the front center of the stage. We ask parents and teacher to please keep children away from this area.  

For those parents who wish to get closer to take your child’s photos (there is really no need since our Club 166 will take professional photos during the performance and your child will be in many of them), there will a marked spot along the corridor, please refrain from approaching the stage as live video production will be in process for the entire show, thank you for our cooperation.

 

Safety and Security
Parents, please keep track of your children. We have hired a police officer to patrol the grounds to be extra safe. Please help us by being vigilant and alert. If you notice anything unusual, please let either the officer or Admin know immediately.

New Exciting Performances This Year

Chinese instrument Erhu 二胡 (all players taught by the renown Erhu instructor, Ms. Qin Qian, 覃茜), Peking Opera Singing 京剧清唱, Ballet 芭蕾 (Maddie Glinski of IBT Academy), Modern Dance 现代舞, Fitness Dance 广场舞, Calligraphy 书法 (visiting artist Mr. Xiao Xingrong and our own Calligraphy teacher Dr. Hsu).  Especially notable this year are our higher grade students, who are super creative and performing in many 语言节目 plays, comedic skits, poetry recitation, and slide presentations! Our show is gearing up to be the most diverse and festive in MLCCC history! Don’t come alone, be sure to invite your friends and neighbors too.
