3. Fundraising from Amazon Smile Purchases – At No EXTRA Cost to You but Benefits MLCCC every time You Buy

If you need to purchase holiday gifts from Amazon.com, you will simultaneously donate to MLCCC at absolutely no cost to you. Here is how it works:

We set up MLCCC as a donation recipient of Amazon Smile. So whenever you buy from Amazon, please support us by buying via their charity portal smile.amazon.com and choosing "Main Line Chinese Culture Center" as your charity.  You only need to choose the charity once, then every subsequent time, use smile.amazon.com.  Smile.amazon.com has the same items at the same prices as amazon.com, but Amazon will donate 0.5% of your purchase price to MLCCC, at no extra cost to you. Thanks, Amazon and Thank you for your support.

 

4. Chinese Book “There Are No Foreign Lands” on Sale at deep-discount of $10 

“There Are No Foreign Lands”《世界无界》is a book written by Jeffery Sheehan, a former Associate Dean of International Relations at UPenn’s Wharton School. Out of his own 13,000+ acquaintances, Sheehan interviews 21 people from 21 countries, thus taking the reader on a global tour to introduce the people whom he believes will lead the world to find common ground with each other and work together productively.

The book is translated into Chinese. The cost is only $10. Quantities are limited. First come first serve at Admin Desk this Sunday.
