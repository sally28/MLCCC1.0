1. Assembly Potluck / Restaurant Food on May 19

We would like to try something different this year and change the Assembly into more of a school-wide event for everyone instead of just the award winners.  It will begin at noon to match lunch, with a “school-wide potluck + restaurant food” in the cafeteria, then awards also in the cafeteria, then Sports Day on the field.  

 

Assembly Schedule:

-12pm - 1pm: Lunch in Cafeteria：

- Start at noon so families who normally cook lunch still cook but brings to school to share while food is still hot.

- School will purchase restaurant food, drinks, dessert to supplement.

- Every family (whether admin, teacher, PTO, or parent) either brings an entree dish or pays a fee.
