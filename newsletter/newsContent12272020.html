4.《环球青少年演说大赛》6-18岁青少年  Super Orator Global Youth Chinese Speech Contest for Ages 6-18

《环球青少年演说大赛》是一场面向世界华裔青少年的全球性演说大赛！第一届大赛活动曾覆盖了80多个国家和地区的华裔群体，成为华语世界当之无愧的语言表达类巅峰盛会！赛事与节目深度联动，为全球华裔青少年提供了展示才能、放飞梦想的平台！对疫情下被迫离群索居的青少年是一个开阔眼界、释放潜能、提升社交能力的好机会！

本次第二届大赛仍由中国华文教育基金会牵头，北京赛酷雅有限公司承办，全美中文学校(CSAUS)再次授权为美国赛区协办单位！

欢迎所有6-18岁青少年参与，无论是否在中文学校上学。

7. “文化中国·2020海外华裔青少年中华文化大赛”线上海外预选赛开始线上报名！Chinese Culture Contest for Overseas Chinese Youth

本次大赛由福建省侨务办公室和华侨大学共同主办，邀请年龄在12-22周岁的海外华裔青少年参加。

本次大赛内容：    分中华文化知识测试和才艺展示两部分。中华文化知识测试主要以“中国历史”、“中国地理”、“中国文化”和时事知识为主要内容，线上考试90分钟；才艺以演讲、朗诵、舞蹈、歌曲、乐器、武术六大类，总时间2至3分钟，采取自行录制上传的形式。

8. 2021 Virtual Chinese New Year Gala Invitation - Call for Performance Videos Submission

Due to the Covid Pandemic's uncertainties and the ultimate safety concerns for our community, MLCCC’s traditional Chinese New Year Gala does not look feasible once again this coming spring. So, we are planning a virtual Gala for the forthcoming 2021 Chinese New Year celebration. We are calling for performance video submissions for consideration of inclusion in the virtual Gala presentation. Since it is the celebration of the Chinese new year, which exemplifies our culture and traditions, contents related to the Chinese New Year festival, culture, customs will be preferred but not required.

We can not guarantee that all submissions will be included for the final compilation due to the video length limitation and depending on the total number of submissions and video quality. However, your video may be considered for our Podcast and Vlog publications later on, pending quality and contents acceptance. This invitation is voluntary, however, we encourage everyone, especially our students, young and old,  to take part in this special event, and to showcase their learning at MLCCC school and camp.

Please note that your contents will be broadcasted online worldwide;  privacy discretion is advised.

Here are some basic guideline & contents suggestions to consider:

  • Please limit the length of the video to under 3 minutes
  • Try to record in a quiet environment unless it is an outdoor project
  • Try to have good even lighting but no glares on the main subject
  • Test record and check the audio level and video quality
  • The storytelling, recitation, family interview, dialogue, song &  poems, and instrumental music are all welcome (can be in and out of classroom learning) (比如：相声、两三个同学的小品剧、个人/集体诗朗诵、采访，小魔术、剪纸，儿童游戏表演， 小“绝活”，器乐演奏， 书法， 对联， 等等都可以考虑, 中国春节文化， 包罗万象）
  • Unique contents not mentioned above can be considered per inquiry.

Our goal is to celebrate both the New Year and our students’ accomplishments during this unprecedented year. Winter break is a perfect time to work on this project. Feel free to email us if you have any questions/requests.

  • Content-Suitable Business Ads (or your good wishes) are welcome too (service fee may apply), but the length must be no longer than 30 seconds. Contents discretion is advised, please email us for details. Thanks in advance for your support of MLCCC!
