Dear Parents,

 

As the Coronavirus threat in Wuhan and China continues to improve, 

We now face an uphill battle in the USA, the country we live in and call home. 

Philadelphia is the next COVID-19 “Hot Spot” in the country according to the CDC’s recent predictions, we must be ready, we are all in this together! United we Stand!

Penn Medicine will be leading the way in this battle and the frontline medical staff are facing a critical shortage of protective medical PPE supplies such as N95 masks. They need our help! They need our help badly!

 

Penn Medicine has been very supportive of MLCCC in the past (with 4 grants in the last 5 years and being a generous Gala sponsor several years in a row). 

Now it is our turn to show that we care too as a community!

 

We are still collecting donations in support of Penn Medicine's frontline fight against the coronavirus. 

Please join us, make a donation and make a real difference as we face this pandemic together. Please also ask if your company matches your donations.

2, Fundraising and PPE Donation Campaign Update as of April 10 

Since February, we have collected $4,810 from Facebook, $1,790 from PayPal, totaling $6,600 as of April 10! (of which $1,450 is for Wuhan and the rest for Penn Medicine), plus some PPEs people donated. Our donation was already used to make purchases of nearly 6000 masks from China, we have received one of three packages already, together with donated PPEs from our partners and MLCC families, we will be able to deliver almost 10,000 masks to Penn Medicine as soon as we receive the rest of the packages. see below for the complete list of generous donors in this campaign, thank you!

 

In Addition to our ongoing monetary donation campaign, MLCCC has been working with devoted individuals of the Chinese American community of the greater Philadelphia (医院对接援助) and caring individuals and organizations in MainLand China to facilitate several direct shipments/drop-off of PPEs (N95, Surgical masks, Goggles, gowns, etc), cumulatively estimated to be in the range of 30,000 or more masks (the process is still ongoing, the final tally will be available in the future), to the Department of Materials Management at Penn Medicine, Temple Hospital, Jefferson Hospital, Einstein Hospital, and much more. Those PPEs have already made a difference in protecting the frontline doctors and nurses in combating the COVID-19 pandemic. 

Please watch out for news reports of the PPEs donation campaign initiated by many Chinese American organizations/communities across America, we are proud to help out and to be a part of the healing process because we are all in this together!

 

Thank you to so many of you who have donated! Please see the list of all donors below. United we stand!

 

3. Steps to Increase Online Security in ZOOM

Whether we use Zoom as parents or teachers, we should all practice the following security measures: 

- Keep our Zoom software up to date. Frequently check for updates. Set up automatic updates if you can. 

- Do not share/post the Zoom links in a public forum.

- Have your children use real names to sign in. Teachers will not admit an unfamiliar name in the classroom.  

- Please read the April 2 Special Email from school for more tips to secure your children’s Zoom participation.
