6.《环球青少年演说大赛》6-18岁青少年  Super Orator Global Youth Chinese Speech Contest for Ages 6-18

本次第二届大赛仍由中国华文教育基金会牵头，北京赛酷雅有限公司承办，全美中文学校(CSAUS)再次授权为美国赛区协办单位！

8. Virtual 2021 Chinese New Year Gala - Call for Performance Videos Submission by Jan. 31, 2021

Due to the uncertainties of the Covid Pandemic and the ultimate safety concerns for our community, MLCCC’s traditional Chinese New Year Gala does not look feasible once again this coming spring. So, we are planning a virtual Gala for the forthcoming 2021 Chinese New Year celebration. We are calling for performance video submissions for consideration of inclusion in the virtual Gala presentation. Since it is the celebration of the Chinese new year, which exemplifies our culture and traditions, contents related to the Chinese New Year festival, culture, customs will be preferred.

We can not guarantee all submissions for the final inclusion due to the limitation on the video length and depending on the total number of submissions and video quality. However, your video may be considered for our Podcast and Vlog publications later on, pending quality and contents acceptance. This invitation is voluntary, however, we encourage everyone, especially our students, young and old,  to take part in this special event, and to showcase their learning at MLCCC school and camp.

Please note that your contents will be broadcasted online worldwide;  privacy discretion is advised.

Here are some basic guideline & contents suggestions:

  • Please limit the length of the video to under 3 minutes
  • Try to record in a quiet environment unless it is an outdoor project.
  • Try to have good even lighting but no glares on the main subject.
  • Test record and check the audio level and video quality
  • The storytelling, recitation, family interview, dialogue, song &  poems, and instrumental music are all welcome (can be in and out of classroom learning) (比如：相声、两三个同学的小品剧、个人/集体诗朗诵、采访，小魔术、剪纸，儿童游戏表演， 小“绝活”，器乐演奏， 书法， 对联， 等等都可以考虑, 中国春节文化， 包罗万象）
  • Unique contents not mentioned above can be considered per inquiry..

Our goal is to celebrate both the New Year and our students’ accomplishments during this unprecedented year. Winter break is a perfect time to work on this project. Feel free to email us if you have any questions/requests.

  • Content-Suitable Business Ads (or your good wishes) are welcome too (service fee may apply), but the length must be no longer than 30 seconds. Contents discretion is advised, please email us for details. Thanks in advance for your support of MLCCC!
