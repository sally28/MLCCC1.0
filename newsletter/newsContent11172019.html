3. Thanksgiving Tree of Gratitude

Our PTO will be continuing MLCCC Thanksgiving Tradition this year with a Tree of Gratitude. Children are encouraged to write “thankful” notes (can be in English or Chinese) and paste onto a big picture of a tree.  This activity teaches our children to be appreciative of their parents, teachers, friends, and environment in general.

5. Speech Contest Moved to Spring

In response to parents’ feedback on the busy holiday season of December for our children (many recitals and performances) and the busy month of January (Chinese New Year Gala), we are moving our Speech Contest to February or March.   

 

Please note that also per parents’ feedback, two major changes from last year are:

- no video submissions this year; the contest will just be onsite.

- point deduction for speeches that are too short (such as less than 1 minute)

7. People’s Light Panto Pop-Up Participation Signup

Several MLCCC families attended People’s Light Theater’s last preview and took part in their Pop-Up Stage Tryout. If you are interested in participating in their seasonal performances, please reply to this email or sign up at the Admin Desk. Please remember that the child has to be at least eight-years-old. Once we have enough signups, we will go through some routines and get ready for the final performance on stage. This year’s Panto is “Little Red Robin Hood”. 


People’s Light’s Panto has been a holiday tradition and a community favorite for many years. With rave reviews and plenty of media coverage/exposure, it is a great program for our children to participate in. 

 

Look forward to seeing everyone this Sunday!
