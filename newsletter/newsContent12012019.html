MLCCC Weekly Newsletter (20191201)

 

Dear Parents, thank you!

We are thankful to each and everyone in our community!

 

We are thankful to our dear teachers, 

For your unselfish dedication to nurturing our children, year in and year out.

 

We are thankful to our dear PTOs and parents,

For your endless, unwavering and unselfish support to our organization for helping create a safe and happy place for our children to grow and learn.

 

We are thankful to our teams of TAs,

For caring and loving your younger brothers and sisters, and your energy and spirit of volunteerism, you help teachers make the classroom a better place to learn, play and grow. 


We are thankful to our grandparents,

For joining our community and sharing your enduring love of our heritage and culture with our parents and children.

 

We are thankful to our dear friends,

For sharing our mission and your long-time support to make MLCCC part of this great community.

 

Most of all, We are thankful to our lovely children,

For your efforts to learn our heritage, language, and culture, and for your efforts to inherit, to share, and appreciate our heritage.

 

The Admin team is proud to serve this great organization and We are thankful to have each other's and your support. 

 

Happy Thanksgiving!

感恩节快乐！
