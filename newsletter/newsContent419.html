Dear Parents,

 

As the Coronavirus threat in Wuhan and China continues to improve, 

We now face an uphill battle in the USA, the country we live in and call home. 

Philadelphia is the next COVID-19 “Hot Spot” according to the CDC’s recent predictions, we must be ready, we are all in this together!

Penn Medicine will be leading the way in this battle and the front-line medical staff are facing a critical shortage of protective medical PPE supplies such as N95 masks. They need our help! They need our help badly!

 

Penn Medicine has been very supportive of MLCCC in the past (with 4 grants in the last 5 years and being a generous Gala sponsor several years in a row). 

Now it is our turn to show that we care too as a community!

 

We are still collecting donations in support of Penn Medicine's 

front-line fight against the Coronavirus. 

Please join us, make a donation and make a real difference as we face this pandemic together. Please also ask if your company matches your donations.

1. Fighting Together: PPEs Delivered to Penn Medicine! Plus Media Coverage

This past week, we delivered almost 10,000 masks to Penn Medicine! Check out media coverage of our donation by

- Philadelphia Inquirer 

- MSN.com powered by Microsoft News

- MLCCC WeChat Account Platform (in Chinese)

- Metro Chinese Weekly (海华都市报) 

- China Press (侨报) (in Chinese, attached) or read it online here.

-and 6ABC and NBC10’s evening news on April 15 (we requested tape from the TV stations and will share with you when we have a copy)

 

As of April 16, we have collected $4,835 from Facebook, $1,790 from PayPal, totaling $6,625 (of which $1,450 is for Wuhan).   

 

In Addition to our ongoing monetary donation campaign, MLCCC has coordinated with devoted individuals of the Chinese American community of Greater Philadelphia (医院对接援助) and caring individuals and organizations of mainland China to facilitate several direct shipments of PPEs (N95, Surgical masks, Goggles, gowns, etc), cumulatively estimated to be in the range of 30,000-50,000 masks. This donation process is still ongoing, we have just received a total of 15,000 masks on behalf of our collaborator, CAPVISION Elite Lt.,  yesterday, and the final tally will be available in the future), to the Department of Materials Management at Penn Medicine, Temple Hospital, Jefferson Hospital, Einstein Hospital, and much more. Those PPEs have already made a difference in protecting the front-line doctors and nurses in combating COVID-19 pandemic. We are proud to help out. We are all in this together!

 

Also, Principal Qunbin Xiong took part in the Red Cross Blood Drive earlier in response to COVID-19 urgency and was interviewed and featured in the Penn Medicine News recently. 

 

Penn Medicine has and will continue to recognize the support by the MLCCC and the Chinese American community of greater Philadelphia.

 

Thank you to so many of you who have donated! Please see list of all donors below. United we stand!

4. ZOOM - Update Software, Use Real Name

For everyone of us Zooming:
