1. Vlog #10 - Lantern Festival

The Lantern Festival marks the final day of celebration of Chinese New Year.  People eat sticky rice balls on that day and put up lanterns with riddles hanging on them to solve.  Let’s listen to Allison Tang and Ada Li tell us more (in Chinese) about this special day in Chinese tradition https://youtu.be/QlnodfiHtWg.

After you watch this special Vlog presentation, you can visit this site with your children to learn more about this important traditional holiday.

6. FREE Mental Wellness Class (顺当) - Improve Your Intellectual Health

Welcome to the FREE Mental Wellness Class (顺当) at MLCCC - Improve Your Intellectual Health with us.

Parents and friends are invited to join the FREE five-sessions Class of MLCCC’s Mental Wellness (via ZOOM).  Join the conversation, and exchange questions about five themes that support and explore mental health well-being.  This class will have an interactive modality, with the possibility of following up in between sessions with behavioral practices and exploring strategies to promote well-being as an individual and a community.

健康的精神心理日常生活方式会帮助你更好的享受人生， 做更合格的家长。

思考发现更好的自己, 达到心理健康新高度, 发现和分享你的特长, 终身学习与提高自己, 善于解决问题和冲突.

欢迎大家加入明朗中国文化中心为大家提供的免费的精神心理健康辅导班。请大家扫描/注册，以便接收课堂及有关信息。

Topics include:

-Mental Wellness; how do we cope? Discussion and practice of body sensing practices (Body/Scan) as a source of support.

-Anxiety, a friend or enemy?

-Implications of Cultural and Domestic violence

-Depression in the context of Coronavirus and family life.

-Open Floor Topic, requested by community members

Starting Date: Sunday, February 28, 2021.

Time: 3:00pm - 4:00pm.

Format: ZOOM online

Instructor: Ms. Christine Gorigoitia, Psy. D. (Trained and Licensed Professional)

Advance registration is required (Zoom class info will be shared via email ONLY)

This Class is in part supported by a grant from Penn Medicine CAREs, thanks Penn Medicine!

7.《环球青少年演说大赛》6-18岁青少年正在报名 Registrations Open for the 2021 Super Orator Global Youth Chinese Speech Contest for Ages 6-18

《环球青少年演说大赛》是一场面向世界华裔青少年的全球性演说大赛！第一届大赛活动曾覆盖了80多个国家和地区的华裔群体，成为华语世界当之无愧的语言表达类巅峰盛会！赛事与节目深度联动，为全球华裔青少年提供了展示才能、放飞梦想的平台！对疫情下被迫离群索居的青少年是一个开阔眼界、释放潜能、提升社交能力的好机会！

本次第二届大赛仍由中国华文教育基金会牵头，北京赛酷雅有限公司承办，全美中文学校(CSAUS)再次授权为美国赛区协办单位！

欢迎所有6-18岁青少年参与，无论是否在中文学校上学。

报名 2020.12.01 - 2021.03.31

海选 2021.03.01 - 2021.03.31

地区赛 2021.04.21 - 2021.05.22

总决赛2021年7月在希腊举办。

报名费：$30 or ¥200 via PayPal or 微信 (明朗现任学员的报名费将由“李松中国文化基金“支付，加油！）

请认准全美中文学校协会海报二维码！（见附件）扫码海报即可观看报名引导视频、大赛宣传视频。

Please note that the contest also includes a division for CSL students, 第二届大赛开设非华裔青少年友谊赛, though all of the instructions we have seen are in Chinese.  

To encourage participation in Chinese language and cultural events, MLCCC will use the Song Li China Culture Foundation’s funds to cover the registration fee of this contest for our currently enrolled students. Best of luck!

1. “Looking Forward To Our New Normal” By Minding Your Mind

The pandemic has presented us with many challenges including fear, isolation, and loneliness. What happens when we head out into a post-pandemic world? How do we lay the groundwork to focus on our own self care? Do we know what it means to acknowledge when we are not okay? What can we do to seek help as we adjust to the new normal?

Please join Sheila Gillin, Minding Your Mind Clinical Director, for a discussion about the challenges ahead for all of us as we transition back to work, back to school, and back to socializing and interacting in person.

This will be a true conversation—come with any questions, concerns, or hopes that you want to discuss with other parents!
