4. Assembly Day Potluck Extravaganza on May 19: End Our School Year on a High Note! Call For Potluck Sign-up

We would like to try something different this year and change the Assembly into more of a schoolwide event for everyone instead of just the award winners.  It will begin at noon to match lunch, with a “school-wide potluck + restaurant food” in the cafeteria, then awards also in the cafeteria, then Sports Day on the field.  We hope everyone can join us!

 

In case you haven't got time or made up your mind signing up for this year's Potluck yet, you are thinking too much, it is not like learning Mandarin for God's Sake. Don't overanalyze it.

 

Your dish doesn't have to be stylish, doesn’t have to have a name, didn't even have to have an origin, as long as it is your favorite or your children's favorite, it will be good enough to share with the super-sized family (MLCCC).

 

Imagine, how it feels when parents tell you they love your dish too, and better yet, ask for a recipe. And you may find another family cooks the dish the same way, you have just found your luck, a pot of luck!

Besides, what fun to join everyone, trying dishes from all over the world, and your children will be so proud of you, and of hearing nice things about your dish. And most importantly, let known your sharing spirits!

 

So, go ahead, Sign up!  show the world your passion at home before it is too late.

When the potluck ends, you will be so happy you signed up.

See you all at the Pot of Luck feast!

 

Assembly Day Schedule:

12pm - 1pm: Lunch in Cafeteria:

- Start at noon so families who normally cook lunch still cook but brings to school to share while food is still hot.

- The school will purchase restaurant food, drinks, dessert to supplement.

- Every family (whether admin, teacher, PTO, or parent) either brings an entree dish or pays a fee.  

Sign up link is here.  Please also tell us about your dishes too as we are all from different locales, it will add to the enjoyment and help our children learn about the vast food traditions in China.

If you don’t bring a dish, then the fee is $10 for ages 12 and older, $5 for ages 6-11, free for age 5 and younger.

- Families sit by class (recommended) so parents and teachers can chat while enjoying the “Potluck” dishes, hand back exams, answer your questions, discuss next year. What a feeling to enjoy each other’s company on the last school day!

 

1pm - 2:30pm: Awards Will be in Cafeteria, NOT in the Auditorium! We have many awards to give out to all the deserving students, PTOs and Parents! Join us to cheer and congratulate them all!

- Many awards to student top performers in various contests, they made us so proud!

- Many awards for PTOs,  TAs, Teachers, Volunteers, and Parents for their time and effort to make all possible!

2:30pm - 4:00pm: Sports Day on Field: Promised to be a Fun-filled day!

PTO and some spirited parents have been planning and organizing this event. It has come down the best 12 suitable games for the young, and those young-at -hearts!  We promise, by the time our sports day is over, you will be short of both breath and tears because the fun of the game is going to be so overwhelmingly filled with waves of laughter, you all will walk away light-hearted for the summer break!
