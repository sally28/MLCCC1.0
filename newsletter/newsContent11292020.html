1. Thank You, We Are Thankful And Grateful For You!
What a year it has been. The words "turbulent" and “unpredictable” have become a part of our daily living vocabularies. We have endured the “Unthinkable” (canceled Ready-To-Go Chinese New Year Gala, Program Book already printed) to the seemingly “Impossible” (closed school and switched to ZOOM Weekend School and ZOOM Summer Camp). Through it all, Our Community has found unique ways to teach, to learn, to connect and support each other in a socially-distanced world.
 
We are proud of our collective capacity to cope, adapt, and grow! We are grateful that you have been a part of our community through it all! Thank you to everyone for your commitment to the Main Line Chinese Culture Center; thank you for being there for our children/students; thank you to our teachers, our parents, our generous friends!

3. Thanksgiving Weekend Challenge - Record Video/Audio Content 
Since school is closed this weekend, here is a challenge for all students/parents. During this special Thanksgiving, many families can’t be together to celebrate, however, you can share the holiday of gratitude and thankfulness via ZOOM or a cell phone.
 
We ask students to interview your parents, especially your grandparents, or relatives/friends from a distance and submit a short video or audio to our Club 166 or Podcast for possible consideration of being included in our upcoming episodes of Vlog and/or Podcast.
This will be a great opportunity for our children to practice their Mandarin learning. 
The preferred language is Mandarin but not a requirement.
Here are some basics in order to record quality video/audio content:
Topics: Thanksgiving Under a Pandemic 疫情下的感恩节
每个视频不超过三分钟, Each video no longer than 3 min
横屏拍摄, Landscape recording
尽量平稳, Try to have camera stabilized
充足光线, Plenty of lighting is critical
避免噪音, Minimize background noise
提交链接, Upload Link: https://forms.gle/DxwWpU9L1mBWSv7w7

5. “文化中国·2020海外华裔青少年中华文化大赛”线上海外预选赛即将开始！Chinese Culture Contest For Overseas Chinese Youth
本次大赛由福建省侨务办公室和华侨大学共同主办，邀请年龄在12-22周岁的海外华裔青少年参加。2016年全美中文学校协会代表队在北京总决赛中获得第五名，2018年在厦门总决赛中获得第四名的好成绩！今年全美中文学校协会再次受邀承办在美国的初赛，希望有更多的会员学校组织学生参赛。
 
本次大赛内容：    分中华文化知识测试和才艺展示两部分。中华文化知识测试主要以“中国历史”、“中国地理”、“中国文化”和时事知识为主要内容，线上考试90分钟；才艺以演讲、朗诵、舞蹈、歌曲、乐器、武术六大类，总时间2至3分钟，采取自行录制上传的形式。
 
大赛时间：   海外预选赛12月底前完成报名，1月20日前完成考试和比赛。
 目前主办方的大赛正式文件尚未发出，各校可先行组织学生学习。
登陆中国华文教育网可查找新版三常知识：http://www.hwjyw.com/textbook/jcxz.shtml
 
中华文化大赛复习题库网址：http://www.hwjyw.com/indexbak_2019.html。
欲购“三常”书印刷本，请电邮 in...@mlccc.org， 明朗现在有少量的库存，先来后到。
 具体报名办法及考试时间等详细信息，待我们收到大赛正式文件后再行通知。

3) Call for Chinese American Women Volunteers for an Interview: My Personal Journey During the COVID Pandemic
 华二代立志从事亚裔研究，请明朗社区的华裔女士们支持：
 
Amherst Colleg四年级学生招募华裔女性志愿者接受访谈，她正在研究美国不同代际的华裔女性在新冠疫情期间的经历。
 
要求：自我认同为华裔美国人（不一定来自大陆), 童年或成年时移民到美国，欢迎各个州, 各种职业和教育背景的华裔女士们参与。
访谈时间一到两小时，将在11月-12月内通过Zoom或电话进行。访谈用英文进行，有需要时可以转为中文表达，访谈之后可能会有后续问题。受访者将完全匿名，一旦研究完成，会将研究结果分享给您。
