1. MLCCC Chinese New Year Gala Submission Details - Extended Deadline Feb. 7

Submission Deadline:

Extended to Feb. 7. Thank you to our Production team Club 166, who gave themselves less time to work so parents can have an extra weekend to submit.  We wanted to do our best to encourage participation. We have a variety of programs already.  Come join the fun!  It’s not a contest but a memorable experience forever captured by our virtual stream.    

Camera/Video Recording Guidelines:

1) Shoot horizontally with maximum resolution.

2) Fill the screen with the performer(s) as much as possible (choose half-length or full-body depending on the program; if shooting dance, please shoot in the largest possible room/space; fix the camera position but can rotate the camera to follow the shot).

3) Use a tripod or other stable support to shoot at the performer's eye level. (This guideline depends on the program content, for example, for calligraphy writing, the camera will point at the paper.)

4) Shoot during the daytime. (Recommend shooting indoors, during a sunny day with plenty of light, but not in direct sunlight.)

5) Simple and clean background, low ambient noise. (Background can be customized according to the content of the program. Also, consider having Chinese New Year ornaments in the background.)

For special circumstances, please feel free to contact Club 166 president, Mr. Li Lu, at Luec...@gmail.com

Content Guidelines/Suggestions: Be creative and be yourself!

  • Please limit the length of the video to under 3 minutes.
  • Storytelling, recitation, family interview, dialogue, song & poems, and instrumental music are all welcome (can be from classroom learning or extracurriculars) (比如：相声、两三个同学的小品剧、个人/集体诗朗诵、采访、小魔术、剪纸、儿童游戏表演、 小“绝活”、器乐演奏、书法、对联等等都可以考虑，中国春节文化，包罗万象。）

However, we encourage everyone, especially our students, young and old, to take part in this special event, to showcase their talents and learning at MLCCC. 明朗中文播客 第10集 《相声集 - 中餐馆名字趣谈》

在美国，从几百个人的小镇，到几百万人的大都市，不管走到哪儿，都会看见中餐馆。从远处一看，挂个红灯笼，竖个“楼”字招牌，晚上十点过后还亮着灯的，肯定是中餐馆。

可是，中餐馆是如何取它们的名字的，我们来听一下唐语菲和唐思文同学做的《相声集 - 中餐馆名字趣谈》，一定可以学到新知识。下一次看到《翠花楼》的，十有八九是北方菜；要想吃辣的，进了《桃香居》，就得叫苦连天啦。

5.《环球青少年演说大赛》6-18岁青少年  Super Orator Global Youth Chinese Speech Contest for Ages 6-18

《环球青少年演说大赛》是一场面向世界华裔青少年的全球性演说大赛！第一届大赛活动曾覆盖了80多个国家和地区的华裔群体，成为华语世界当之无愧的语言表达类巅峰盛会！赛事与节目深度联动，为全球华裔青少年提供了展示才能、放飞梦想的平台！对疫情下被迫离群索居的青少年是一个开阔眼界、释放潜能、提升社交能力的好机会！

本次第二届大赛仍由中国华文教育基金会牵头，北京赛酷雅有限公司承办，全美中文学校(CSAUS)再次授权为美国赛区协办单位！

欢迎所有6-18岁青少年参与，无论是否在中文学校上学。
