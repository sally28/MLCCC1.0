MLCCC Dance Troupe has been enjoying rave reviews recently from the greater Philly community. It is in part because we have an energetic and professional dance teacher, Ms. Ma, Weiwei. In addition to teaching at our school, Ms. Ma is a professional dancer at the “Kun-Yang Lin/ Dancers” company. She and her dance colleagues travel across the country to perform professionally.
