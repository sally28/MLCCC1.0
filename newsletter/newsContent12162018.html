1. Shop AmazonSmile and Support MLCCC, You shop, Amazon Gives, Hassle-free.

AmazonSmile is a website operated by Amazon with the same products, prices, and shopping features as Amazon.com. The BIG difference is that when you shop on AmazonSmile, the AmazonSmile Foundation will donate 0.5% of the purchase price of eligible products to the charitable organization of your choice - MLCCC.

 

All you have to do is go to this website: https://smile.amazon.com/, or click on the Link at MLCCC website homepage, log in, and designate the Main Line Chinese Culture Center as your favorite charity. Every time you shop at AmazonSmile, MLCCC will benefit. The more families take part, the more donation MLCCC will receive from AmazonSmile.

Please take advantage of hassle-free AmazonSmile for your holiday shopping while supporting MLCCC. Thank you!

3. PTO’s “Book Exchange & Sale” Continues This Week in Admin Hallway

1) All transactions are done through “tickets”.  

 

2) To buy books: first buy tickets beforehand,  at $1 = 4 tickets.

 

3) To sell books:

- You decide on how many books to sell and how many tickets to charge for each book. (PTO's recommendation is to sell fewer than 5 books and each book for no more than 25 tickets.)

- Label each book clearly with # of tickets needed to buy it.  

- Lay out a small box next to your books, so people can place tickets into the box (honor system) to buy your books.

- Take your books and box home every Sunday.

 

4) Tickets not used or left over at the end can be exchanged back into money.
