2. 32 Participants in MLCCC’s 1st 《Song Li Cup Chinese Essay Contest》! 

Sponsored by the Song Li China Culture Foundation (SLCCF).

Bestowed upon MLCCC by a generous donation from Dr. Song Li, the mission of SLCCF is to support MLCCC’s missions to serve the local families of the greater Philadelphia community. This Foundation will be in principle practiced and executed to promote the heritage of Chinese Language & Culture and enhance the understanding & exchange of Chinese culture with others in our community. We will consider multiple venues to maximize the benefits of this fund in serving the families.

3. Chinese Book “There Are No Foreign Lands” on Sale for $10 Only

“There Are No Foreign Lands”《世界无界》is a book written by Jeffery Sheehan, a former Associate Dean of International Relations at UPenn’s Wharton School. Out of his own 13,000+ acquaintances, Sheehan interviews 21 people from 21 countries, thus taking the reader on a global tour to introduce the people whom he believes will lead the world to find common ground with each other and work together productively.

The book is translated into Chinese. The cost is only $10. Quantities are limited. Buy at Admin Desk.
