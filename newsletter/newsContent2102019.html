2. Weekend School Chinese Speech Contest - Submit Speech Video Recording by February 24

Similar to last year, Round 1 will be through video recording.  You can record with a cell phone or fancier equipment. We need to hear and see the presentation clearly.  Round 2 will be at Weekend School in front of judges at a future date. Judging will be on pronunciation, tone, content, fluency, and stage presentation.  

 

Contest Rules:

A. Eligible students: CHL or CSL grade 1 or level 1 and above.

B. Recite from memory. It can be a story, a poem, or a tongue twister, from textbook or child’s own writing.

C. Speech must be shorter than 3 minutes.

D. No background music or stage tools are allowed.


Record and Upload Video: Video record at the very beginning a card with the following info.

Then Upload your videos at this link.  Can upload directly from cell phone (let us know if you have trouble uploading files).
