2. Update on False Fire Alarm Last Sunday

Our class was briefly interrupted by a false fire alarm last Sunday, but we are very thankful that our teachers and parents worked closely with the school Admin team to keep our children clearly instructed and in safety, while Admin team worked swiftly with the GVHS and the Fireman to identify the issue and assess the need to evacuate the facility. It was a test of our collective efforts to deal with a “crisis” when it happened. It proved that we have a good team in place, thank you all.

 

We were quickly notified by the High School that the false fire alarm was caused by a malfunctioning sensor in the ceiling far away from our classroom wing (not a foul play by anyone!).
