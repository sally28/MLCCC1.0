MLCCC School Newsletter #20210404

Dear Parents,

There is No School this Sunday, April 4. Have a joyful and safe Spring Break.

This Sunday, April 4, is also the traditional Chinese Holiday, “清明节“ or “Tomb Sweeping Day”, when we pay our respects to those who have departed and to our ancestors. Here is a poem in Chinese and translated to English that tells the story of this tradition, by 杜牧 Du Mu of the Tang Dynasty. Other Versions of English TRanslation of this poem are here. (It shows you how readers interpret the poem from their own perspective).

MLCCC in the News: MLCCC joined Asian American communities in the Philadelphia area to voice our condemnation of the recent Anti-Asian attacks across America. We stand united and in solidarity with Asian Americans against any form of racial injustice. Specifically, MLCCC took part in the March 28 Stop Anti-Asian Hate Rally in Chinatown on a stormy rainy day. Our student Yeeyee Li, and our Principal Xiong, amongst other MLCCC families, were interviewed by the media.

明朗中文播客 第14集 《童话集 - 【知母草】》

听众朋友们， 大家好。 欢迎来到我们明朗中文播客第14集。 今天是2021年3月26日。 我是伍子颜， 这期明朗中文播客的主持人。今天我们要献给所有大家的是ADA LI 同学朗诵 的故事《知母草》。ADA LI 同学是CHARLESTOWN 小学四年级的学生，她兴趣广泛，平常最喜欢钢琴和花样游泳，还喜欢滑冰，滑雪，芭蕾和尤可里里等。

Ada 讲的真好。这个故事告诉了我们几个道理： 做好事不应该为了私心， 前面两个帮助老婆婆的人都是为了自己的好处， 只有樵夫是真心对待老婆婆， 没有别的目的。  另外， 只有像樵夫一样心肠好的人才会好好用老婆婆交给的本领，帮助更多的人，而不是为了自己赚钱。

6. The Ai Ling Guan Memorial Scholarship Fund - 2021 Application Opens, Due 5/1/2021

Scholarship Description:

Ms. Ai Ling Guan has taught Chinese for over 30 years in several countries. To commemorate her passion for teaching the Chinese language to both children and adults, her family has set up a scholarship fund to honor those teachers and students engaged in the study of the Chinese language in the US and Canada.

Visit https://wuchuanren.org/ to download proper application forms. We encourage all students and teachers to apply for this honorable award and recognition.

Recommendation Letter can be filled out in the form on their website or emailed to gal.sch...@gmail.com

Deadline: May-1-2021

Eligibility:

Students: Students aged 12 – 18 on or before May 1st of the year, studying Chinese at school, weekend /evening Chinese school, or via private tutoring

Teachers: Teachers of the Chinese language or Chinese school administrators

Residing in the US or Canada

The applicant has not previously won the award/scholarship

Awards:

Students: 14 winners, $500 each

Teachers: 3 winners, $1,000 each

Application Process:

Application open online: March 1st

Application due: May 1st

Results announcement: June 1st

Students: Scholarship will be paid directly to the Chinese school/tutor where the student studies Chinese and will serve as tuition. If she/he studies Chinese primarily at a public or private school, or if she/he does not plan to continue studying Chinese, the scholarship can be forwarded to fund other studies

Teachers: Award will be given directly to the winner in the form of a check.

4. Invitation to Watch PBS’s Special Timely Documentary: “Asian Americans''

Thank you to the generosity and support of PBS, the “Asian American” Series will be available again to the public for free, including the Mandarin subtitled companion episodes. Our Principal, Qunbin Xiong, has been part of the Chinese Subtitle Translation Team for this special PBS series. Please take time and watch with your children and learn our history of immigration to the United States and why Asian Americans still face racial injustice and systematic discrimination in America 150 years later.

PBS再一次对大众免费开放了 “亚裔美国人”  纪录片。看过的可以温故而知新，没看过的一定要抽空和孩子们一起看一遍。普及亚裔文化和历史知识要从孩子们的教育做起，先从我们自己了解美国亚裔自己的艰辛的移民历史开始是不让历史重演的最好办法。学习历史，借鉴历史。如果更多人了解了我们美国亚裔的过去，也就会明白当前反对种族歧视和种族仇恨运动的重要性。
