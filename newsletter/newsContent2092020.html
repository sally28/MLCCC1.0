1. School Still On Break This Sunday (02/09)

As indicated by the school-wide survey results, a large majority of our families want the school to still remain closed this Sunday, as we give a little more time to observe any development on the Coronavirus. Thank you to everyone’s response/comments in the survey, and for your patience and understanding. 

 

2. FAQs Related to School Closing

(1) We are not aware of anyone in our community that has any symptoms of the Coronavirus. Jan 26’s Gala and Feb 2’s school were canceled because information on the Coronavirus was just starting to appear then and information was very limited, plus there were limited response and guidance from any U.S. departments, plus the Gala’s Show is held in an enclosed room with hundreds of people, including especially young children and elderly. Additional info on the CDC website.

 

(2) The preliminary plan is to make up for the missed class days by extending the school year and possibly running a class on Easter Sunday. We will update on this later. 

  

(3) Some of our teachers have already emailed review materials to their classes many days ago. More teachers will email review materials or key learning points to their classes within a few days. Please see below for additional online learning resources.

 

3. MLCCC Promotes Our Sponsors Through Multiple Channels

MLCCC’s missions rely on the generous support of our sponsors. Despite our Gala’s cancellation and school closing, we didn’t forget our responsibility to our Gala Sponsors. In addition to the distribution of our already printed Gala book to our community as planned, we have shared the book and sponsors’ ads with our community at large through multiple channels, including the first edition of "MLCCC Chinese New Year Online Gala", our YouTube channel, Facebook, and more. Please watch and share amongst your friends and neighbors. Thank you!
