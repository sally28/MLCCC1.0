4. Seeking Student Announcers,明朗广播电台招聘小小播音员

Requirement: CHL Grade 4 and up, able to speak Mandarin somewhat fluently. 

In an effort to surround our students with opportunities to practice Chinese 学中文，用中文, we are seeking interested students to be announcers on MLCCC Radio. Each week’s newsletter headlines will be written for them in Chinese, they will record it (voice memo function on phones) and send it to us for inclusion in MLCCC Radio. They do not have to do this every week. We have a group of students who are taking turns by the week. If interested, please let us know，this is a great opportunity to help students to practice their mandarin learning. You can even share their radio broadcast with families and friends anywhere and make them feel proud of your students.

B. Temporary Part-Time Job: University of Pennsylvania Libraries - Chinese Technical Services Clerk

 

The Penn Libraries seeks a temporary, part-time, Technical Services Clerk to assist in the processing of Chinese-language print books. Reporting to the Area Studies Technical Services Coordinator and working closely with the Chinese Library Specialist, the Chinese Technical Services Clerk will assist in the technical processing of Chinese language materials for the Penn Libraries in accordance with established workflows, including searching for bibliographic information, updating holdings information, barcoding physical inventory, and clerical duties as required.

 

DUTIES

 

Primary responsibilities may include but are not limited to:

•         Perform routine searches of Penn Libraries catalogs (Franklin & Alma) and external bibliographic utilities (e.g. OCLC Connexion) for bibliographic information.

•         Unboxes newly acquired materials (with occasional lifting of boxes), verifies contents against packing lists, and arranges books on storage shelves.

•         Process physical inventory, including flagging and barcoding of library items, transporting book trucks through Penn Libraries spaces, and maintaining order in shelving areas.

•         Under the direction of the Chinese Library Specialist, update bibliographic, holdings, and items records to assist in the cataloging process.

•         Under direction of the Chinese Library Specialist, perform duplicate checks, supply and verify romanized Chinese text (pinyin) where required, and format invoices in spreadsheets to assist in the acquisitions process.

•         Work collegially and collaboratively with student assistants.

•         Perform additional duties as assigned.

 

QUALIFICATIONS

 

Required Qualifications:

•         Strong knowledge of the written Chinese language in both traditional and simplified scripts.

•         Basic computer skills in a Windows computing environment.

 

Preferred Qualifications:

•         Experience in a library setting working with bibliographic data.

•         An associate’s degree or higher from an accredited institution.

•         Ability to identify problems and willingness to assist in implementing solutions.

•         Experience manipulating spreadsheet data (e.g. Microsoft Excel).

 

Please note: This is a part-time, temporary position. For more information or to apply please contact Mike Williams, Area Studies Technical Services Coordinator at the Penn Libraries, mpw2@upenn.edu (215.573.6398).
