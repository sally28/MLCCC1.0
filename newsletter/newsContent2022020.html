3. MLCCC New Year Gala Online - Some Gala Performances and Sponsor Ads Compiled  

In order to minimize our children’s disappointments from our Gala’s cancellation and still honor our duty to our generous Sponsors, our Video Club compiled their existing footage of our rehearsals and classroom celebrational activities to make our 2020 MLCCC Online Gala (First of its kind).  

Groups featured are New Year Greetings from Clubs, CHL G1 making dumplings, CHL G4 video guide, Crosstalk by Lily Tang & Father, Adult Folk Dance Class, Youth Folk Dance Class, Children Folk Dance Class, Martial Arts, Chorus, and Fitness Club.  Due to the pressing time and availability of video content, we apologize for the partial enclosure.
