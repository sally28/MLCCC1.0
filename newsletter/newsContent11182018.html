1. MLCCC Tea Club Started: “明朗茶社” 正式开业，欢迎光临!

Stop by the MLCCC Tea Club at the Admin Hallway and enjoy a cup of hot tea in this wintry weather!  We promise it will warm your heart and soul right away. A big Thank You to Mrs. Angelia Luo-Lawson and her husband, Mr. Stephen Lawson, for this heartwarming idea and their entire family (her mom and dad) for managing the club, such as bringing in the teapot, sampler teas, and tasty biscuits. You are welcome to bring your favorite teas or biscuits to share with others. Join the Club Wechat group here to share your thoughts with other club members.

 

Through PTO’s persistent and responsive efforts, MLCCC now offers a variety of clubs, parents are welcome to join to stay fit, healthy, or learn a few things or just enjoy each other's company. To find out more, stop by the Admin Desk or talk to any PTO members.

 

2. PTO’s “Wall of Gratitude” (明朗感恩墙) Continues This Sunday, All Welcome.

Messages can be in English, in Chinese, in drawings or pictures, etc. as long as they come from the children’s hearts.  All children are welcome to write and post. Whether they do so during recess or as a classroom activity is up to each teacher.  Thank you to PTO and the teachers for creating and supporting this meaningful event for our children.

We also encourage student or classes to take a memory photo in front of the “WALL” so that you can share this event with your family and friends, or write a photo story on it.

Thank you to those children who already posted, we enjoy reading your grateful messages very much, thank you.

 

3. School Closed on November 25th for Thanksgiving Holiday!  

We wish everyone a very happy Thanksgiving holiday! Travel Safely.

 

4. PTO Sponsored Mr. Wish Comes Intermittently

As the weather gets colder, Mr. Wish will come intermittently (with hot drinks) instead of every week. Check our school email/Wechat regularly for dates/time of their visit.

 

Look forward to seeing everyone this Sunday!
