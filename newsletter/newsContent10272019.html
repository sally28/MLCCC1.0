3. Halloween Parade This Sunday 2:45PM-3:05PM

Please make sure costumes are safe and the children have good visibility.  Certain accessories such as swords, pitchforks, or other fake weapons, are dangerous in a school setting, please do not send these to school.  The focus of this year’s parade will be on costumes and fun spirit instead of treats. Candy and trinkets handed out will be less than prior years’.     

 

Parade route (please follow the yellow parade arrows and be careful of steps): Admin Hallway -> Taichi, turn left, go up steps -> outside big gym, turn left -> main entrance, turn left -> go down steps, back to Admin Hallway, and can go to 3PM class.

 

Each class will walk the parade route as a unit.  The starting point for everyone will be the Admin Hallway, so Martial Arts and Tennis class need to come to the Admin Hallway if you want to join in.  

 

For those students who have class before 3PM, they will be led by their teachers and assisted by homeroom parents and TAs.

 

For those students who normally come to school at 3PM, please arrive this Sunday by 2:40PM if you want to join the parade.      

 

We need many parents along the parade route to help as ushers.  Please meet at the Admin Hallway at 2:30PM if you can help. Thank you!

 

Parents who would like to take photos please spread out along the parade route.

 

Happy Halloween!  To Page Top

6. NEW School Activity: FREE Yoga Class at 3PM

Ms. Esther Estes, our own CSL parent, a retired physician and a dedicated health living advocate, is offering free Yoga class at our school! The first class last Sunday was a great turnout. 


Location/Time: 3PM (3:10PM this Sunday due to parade) on the Second Floor Hallway (take stairs behind the new Admin Desk).

Who can attend: Adults only (seniors are welcome too)

What to bring: A Yoga mat, a towel, a bottle of water and a light heart

 

Ms. Estes is a Registered Power Yoga Teacher (RYT-200) and a member of the Yoga Alliance.  She is currently volunteering at a community center. She is a retired physician focused on advocating healthy living.  She is registered and listed in the Yoga Alliance If you want to learn more about her.


We thank Esther for her volunteering spirit to make MLCCC a better and healthier place! Her class is free but please donate as you wish to support our PTO. To Page Top

8. NEW Activity: Birthday Recognition

Students with September or October birthdays and whose parents have filled the sign-up below, please come to the Admin Desk to pick up your present from our PTO!

 

In order to make our school community more fun for our children, the ladies of our School Store came up with the idea of PTO and Admin teaming up to recognize the birthdays of our students. When the month of a child’s birthday comes around, the child can pick up a gift/prize from the Admin Desk. Parents/Grandparents can also write or record a greeting that will be announced or played on 明朗广播电台 MLCCC Radio (link is also at the bottom of this newsletter). Summer birthdays will be celebrated in May. For privacy concerns, we only need the birth month, not year nor day. If you would like to try this out, please complete the simple sign up here.
