Contest Rules (re-post from March 17 newsletter):

Structure/process is the same as last year, EXCEPT for:

- MLP G5 words are now 马立平的西游记 words. (Reminder that students still need to know all prior grades' words.)

- Reduced number of CSL Level 4 words and created CSL Level 5+ words.

 

Eligibility: at least CHL Grade 1, or CSL Level 1, or equivalent (students not in a language class who wants to participate needs to be assessed and placed).

 

1. Contestants will be tested based on language track, i.e. CHL (MLP, MZHY, AP) or CSL, and grade level.  

 

1A: CHL MLP of CHL MZH

- Most of the words tested will be from their current grade level but some of the words will be from EACH prior grade.

- Read out the words accurately.

 

1B: AP Class students,

- All of the words tested will be from their current grade level.

- Read out the words accurately.

 

1C: CSL,

- All of the words tested will be from their current grade level.

- Read out the words accurately AND translate into English.           

 

2. The contest will be carried out via a PPT (PowerPoint slide show) on a laptop, and the screen automatically advances to the next word after a pre-defined time duration (in seconds). A judge can advance manually if a student finishes a slide faster.

 

3. Partial credit will be given.

美思高与明朗通力合作，打造高中完胜生物课，强调五要素:

重要性：生物课是美高学生的必修课，谁都逃不掉！如果学的不好会直接影响高中最后的总成绩，以致于直接影响能否申请好大学。

难点和痛点：生物课要求有一定的数学，物理和化学的基础，同时又要有相当的阅读理解能力，因为它涉及到计算（难点）和大量的词汇，以及不常用的专业术语（痛点）。

教学症结：美国高中通常很难找到有博士学位的教师去系统教生物，这样导致学生的生物知识支离破碎、不连贯，缺乏系统性。很多的的生物老师要求学生去死记硬背生物术语，但并不能融会贯通。

现实问题：纵观美国大学生物SAT Subject 和AP 考试，里面涵盖的生物知识面比较广，在短时间内很难全面掌握。

 

美思高是解决通道：

美思高有幸请到专业生物老师做系统的培训，弥补学校教学之不足。既避免死记硬背，又能扎扎实实打好生物学基础，还能教会学生如何用学到的生物知识去解决生物的问题。顺利通过SAT Biology subject ，AP Biology 考试，为有兴趣参加生物科学竞赛和生物研究的学生打下坚实的基础。这是美思高的开设此课程的目标。

你准备好奔着这个目标来吗？
