4. Steps to Increase Online Security in ZOOM

With so many people all over the country using Zoom, we are starting to hear of unsavory actions by unsavory people who barge into the Zoom classroom. Zoom Corporation is taking action and sending security modifications in updates. There are a few things we should all do as well, regardless of whether we are a student or teacher: 

- Keep our Zoom software up to date. Frequently check for updates. Set up automatic updates if you can. 

- Do not share/post the Zoom links in a public forum.

- Have your children use real names to sign in. Teachers will not admit an unfamiliar name in the classroom.  

- Please read the earlier special email from school for more tips to secure your children’s zoom participation.
