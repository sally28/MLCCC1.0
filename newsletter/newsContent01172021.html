2. MLCCC Chinese New Year Gala Submission Details - Extended Deadline Feb. 7

Submission Deadline:

Extended to Feb. 7. Thank you to our post-production team Club 166 who gave themselves less time to work so parents can have an extra weekend to submit.  

Camera/Video Recording Guidelines:

1) Shoot horizontally with maximum resolution.

2) Fill the screen with the performer(s) as much as possible (choose half-length or full-body depending on the program; if shooting dance, please shoot in the largest possible room/space; fix the camera position but can rotate the camera to follow the shot).

3) Use a tripod or other stable support to shoot at the performer's eye level. (This guideline depends on the program content, for example, for calligraphy writing, the camera will point at the paper.)

4) Shoot during the daytime. (Recommend shooting indoors, during a sunny day with plenty of light, but not in direct sunlight.)

5) Simple and clean background, low ambient noise. (Background can be customized according to the content of the program. Also, consider having Chinese New Year ornaments in the background.)

For special circumstances, please feel free to contact Club 166 president, Mr. Li Lu, at Luec...@gmail.com

Submission link: https://forms.gle/DxwWpU9L1mBWSv7w7

明朗春晚节目录制要求：

1）相机或手机最大分辨率横屏拍摄；

2）人物尽量充满画面（根据节目内容选择半身或全身，舞蹈请选择最大的空间录制，相机位置固定，但可以转动跟拍）

3）利用三脚架或支撑物在演员平视的位置拍摄（根据节目内容可进行调整，比如书法）

4）白天拍摄（建议晴天中午室内无直射阳光处）

5）背景简洁，避免噪音（根据节目内容可以自定背景，建议考虑有中国春节文化元素的饰物）

特殊情况请WeChat或Email联系李路（Luec...@gmail.com)

提交链接: https://forms.gle/DxwWpU9L1mBWSv7w7

Content Guidelines/Suggestions:

  • Please limit the length of the video to under 3 minutes.
  • Storytelling, recitation, family interview, dialogue, song & poems, and instrumental music are all welcome (can be from classroom learning or extracurriculars) (比如：相声、两三个同学的小品剧、个人/集体诗朗诵、采访、小魔术、剪纸、儿童游戏表演、 小“绝活”、器乐演奏、书法、对联等等都可以考虑，中国春节文化，包罗万象。）
  • Unique contents not mentioned above can be considered per inquiry.
  • Content-Suitable Business Ads (or your good wishes) are welcome too (service fee may apply). But the length must be no longer than 30 seconds. Content discretion is advised, please email us for details. Thanks in advance for your support of MLCCC!

We cannot guarantee all submissions will be included in the final program due to limitations on the total program length, the number of submissions, and video quality. However, your video may be considered for our Podcast and Vlog publishing later on, pending quality and content acceptance. This invitation is voluntary, however, we encourage everyone, especially our students, young and old, to take part in this special event, to showcase their talents and learning at MLCCC. Please note that your contents will be broadcasted online worldwide; privacy discretion is advised.

明朗中文播客 第9集 《访谈集 – 对话【马立平中文】的马立平老师》

2020年12月8日，我们同《马立平中文》的马立平老师进行了一次访谈。一同进行谈话的还有肖文老师，她的女儿唐语菲，儿子唐思文。姐弟俩都是用《马立平中文》教科书学习中文的。

马老师给我们介绍了她的教书和编书的基本思路，学中文一定要认字，拼音仅仅是教发音的工具。我们一定要创造一个学习中文的环境，才会事半功倍。另外，同我们上一集访谈集的许老师一样，希望同学们从中学到中华文化，感谢中华文化，做一个堂堂正正的人。

这一集播客把我们的访谈分享给听众朋友们，希望大家把中文和中华文化学好。下一集播客将于元月29日发布，播出我们同学制作的《相声集 – 中餐馆趣谈》，敬请关注。

6.《环球青少年演说大赛》6-18岁青少年  Super Orator Global Youth Chinese Speech Contest for Ages 6-18

《环球青少年演说大赛》是一场面向世界华裔青少年的全球性演说大赛！第一届大赛活动曾覆盖了80多个国家和地区的华裔群体，成为华语世界当之无愧的语言表达类巅峰盛会！赛事与节目深度联动，为全球华裔青少年提供了展示才能、放飞梦想的平台！对疫情下被迫离群索居的青少年是一个开阔眼界、释放潜能、提升社交能力的好机会！

本次第二届大赛仍由中国华文教育基金会牵头，北京赛酷雅有限公司承办，全美中文学校(CSAUS)再次授权为美国赛区协办单位！

欢迎所有6-18岁青少年参与，无论是否在中文学校上学。

1. MLK Day Virtual Volunteer Opportunity: Get Involved, Get Inspired!

On Monday, January 18 from 1-3 PM ET, join Philly Counts, the Office of Immigrant Affairs, and the Mayor’s Office of Public Engagement, in coordination with the Philadelphia Department of Public Health, for a COVID-19 check-in phone bank.

We will be calling residents in key city underserved neighborhoods to check in on them during the pandemic. We will also be asking how people feel about receiving the vaccine once it is available. This information is critical for informing the next steps for COVID-19 community support and vaccine distribution.

Training will be offered at the start of the phone bank and you can make calls in English, Spanish, or Mandarin. All are welcome to participate in our virtual service activity. Sign up today!

MLCCC TAs (high school students) are especially welcome and encouraged to take part. This is a great opportunity to get involved and make a difference for the betterment of our community without leaving your home. Sign up now, get trained, learn about the COVID-19 vaccine, and reach out to the local families.
