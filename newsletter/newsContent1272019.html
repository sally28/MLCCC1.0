Special Live Event This Sunday Only:

Mr. Xiao, Xingrong, the renown visiting Chinese Calligrapher from Hubei, China, is back at MLCCC again, He will be writing Chinese Couplet for parents during the school hour.

Mr. Xiao’s calligraphic work is highly praised in China and has received numerous national awards and recognition in his career, his writing is highly sought after and collectible in China. He even brought his own ink and brushes with him from China only for this special occasion.

To make his time worthwhile, and to help with MLCCCC”s PTO and future cultural events, we ask parents to please consider a donation for his work, with a suggested minimum donation of $3.00, more is most welcome.

 

1. Call for Volunteers and Donations for Gala and Culture Fair

Volunteers

We need help throughout Gala day Feb 3: in the morning to setup/decorate, in the afternoon to supervise stations during the Culture Fair, and to clean up afterward, etc.  Please sign up for a slot in this link and help make our Gala the most fun for our children!  

 

Snack Donation

Whether store-bought or homemade, please consider bringing a snack/treat to the School Store to sell during Gala. Proceeds go to support future school-wide events. Thank you.

 

Funds Donation

We always welcome donations throughout the year to support our popular School Store, PTO, dance troupes, Video Club, Photo Club, and much more!   

Look out for red donations boxes throughout the Gala and Fair.  You can also hand deliver to PTO or Admin. If you don’t carry cash, no problem. We have many other ways for you to donate, such as Paypal (link on MLCCC homepage), shopping at AmazonSmile, Venmo (our ID: MLCCC2018), checks, company match or grant.  Every dollar counts and makes a real difference. Thank you in advance!

 

2. Red Envelopes (压岁钱 Yāsuìqián) for Every Student!

School and PTO have prepared red envelopes of a special New-Year edition of 10 school dollars for every student. We are following a long Chinese tradition to treat every child during the new year.

- If you are a student in a Chinese language class (CHL or CSL), the Chinese History class, or SAT, please get them from your teacher.  

- If you are not in any of the above classes, please come to the Admin Desk to get yours.  

Happy Chinese New Year! 新年快乐 (Xīnnián kuàilè)！

 

3. Gala Day Highlights

School has purchased pizza to arrive at 11:30AM as lunch for everyone onsite, whether you are a volunteer, a performer, a student or a parent. You are here to make a difference, you deserve a slice of pizza from us, thank you!

 

Ticket for Free Treat at Sign In

New this year, sponsored by the PTO (thank you!), when you sign in at the door on Gala Day, you will receive a ticket for a free treat at the School Store table.  So share this news with everyone so that no one misses out.

 

4. New and Quality Performances This Year (1:00PM - 3:30PM)

Chinese instrument Erhu 二胡 (all players taught by the renown Erhu instructor, Ms. Qin Qian, 覃茜), Peking Opera Singing 京剧清唱, Ballet 芭蕾 (IBT Academy), Modern Dance 现代舞, Fitness Dance 广场舞, Calligraphy 书法 (visiting artist Mr. Xiao Xingrong and our own teacher Dr. Robert Hsu).  Especially notable this year are our higher grade students, who are super creative and performing in many 语言节目 plays, comedic skits, poetry recitation, and slide presentations! Our show is gearing up to be the most diverse and festive in MLCCC history! Don’t come alone, be sure to invite your friends and neighbors too.
