1. Enjoy PTO’s “Wall of Gratitude” (明朗感恩墙) This Sunday, Thanks for Your Participations.

The 2018 MLCCC’s the great “Wall of Gratitude” will be displayed in the Admin Hallway one last time this Sunday. There are still few spaces for more postings if your children haven’t done so.  We invite everyone to come read our children’s heartfelt and thankful messages, and feel free to take pictures with the Wall. We will also choose a few unique and touching messages to post on MLCCC’s Wechat Public later. See a preview here.

 

2.Interest in 24 Game?

We wanted to ask if students are still interested in participating in the mathematical 24 Game (applying addition, subtraction, multiplication, division to 4 numbers to get 24), which has traditionally been held in January.  If only a few students are interested, we will not hold it.

 

3. MLCCCC PTO Photo Club Special Offer: Family Portrait Service

As we approach the holiday season, MLCCC’s renown Photo Club is proud to provide professional family portrait taking, with the top quality of equipment and services including unlimited self-guided dress/pose changes. The club will provide you with up to 10 digital photos. Come and try out this Pro quality service at an amateur price. See the attached Poster for sample poses and more details or stop by the Admin Desk with your questions.

 

4. All PTO Clubs Opens for Regular Activities:

All PTO Clubs open for activities this Sunday including Photo, Taiji, Fitness and Square Dance (广场舞), Tea Club, and all Sports Clubs. Come and join us for a healthy mind and body, and most importantly, walk away with a great feeling for a week.
