A.Magic Dice by Prof. Po-Shen Loh | Philadelphia, PA | Jan 18th, 2020

罗教授终于要来到费城啦！

By renowned mathematician Po-Shen Loh, this event is for families of 4–8th graders who are interested in developing strong math skills.

 

Prof. Po-Shen Loh is a math enthusiast and evangelist who travels the world and gives fun, inspiring and insightful talks, to increase everyone’s interest and ability in mathematics. He is a math professor at Carnegie Mellon University, the founder of the free online learning platform Expii, and the national coach of the USA Math Olympiad team. His research and educational outreach take him to cities across the world, reaching over 10,000 people each year through public lectures and events, and he has featured in or co-created videos totaling over 5 million YouTube views.

On Jan 18, he will give a math talk in Philadelphia, PA. This event is for all 4-8th grade students and their parents. Prof. Loh will talk about interesting mathematics, and about how to develop mathematical talent, in the hope of planting the seeds of math interest in as many people as possible.

Thanks to the sponsorship of Prof. Loh's online course series Daily Challenge with Po-Shen Loh, and the host Northeast Academy of Philadelphia (NEAP), this event is free to all attendees.

 

Agenda (local time)

 

11:30 – 12:00 pm: Arrival and check-in

12:00 – 13:00 pm: A fun math topic for 4th–8th-grade students and parents

13:00 – 1:15 pm: A brief discussion with parents about education philosophy, sharing pointers to learning resources

1:15 – 1:30 pm: Open Q&A for parents and students on any topics

 

知名美籍华裔数学家，教育家，卡耐基梅隆大学数学系副教授，科技教育公司Expii Inc创始人兼CEO，美国奥林匹克数学国家队总教练，罗博深教授，在费城东北中文学校的组织和邀请下，将于1月18日农历新年到来之际在费城地区进行数学活动，与孩子和家长们开展一次趣味横生的数学之旅！

欢迎所有年龄在4-8年级的小朋友和家长们报名参加 席位不多，想要报名的同学和家长一定抓住机会哦！
